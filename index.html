The Project

When we think of average life expectancy, we think of health, but the truth is that there are more elements in the equation that can be crucial. How can we identify these elements and analyze them? What are the societal factors that most influence the life expectancy of a citizen? In which ones is there a possibility of immediate intervention? And finally, once defined, what is their distribution in time and space?

These are the questions that we propose to deepen with the implementation of this project.
